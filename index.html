Methodology note: 2025 rows are computed from raw HubSpot recipient-event data — opens/clicks are capped to the first 14 days after send, and bots are filtered using our own best-effort rule (not HubSpot's built-in bot exclusion). 2026+ rows use HubSpot's own "excluding bots" snapshot totals pulled from exported reports, with no fixed time window. The two methodologies are not perfectly apples-to-apples — treat cross-year comparisons directionally.
